Attractions

  • Magens Bay Beach
    		•  Located on the north end of St. Thomas, U.S. Virgin Islands, Magens Bay Park is a tropical paradise offering stunning beaches, gorgeous native trees and flowers, and unmatched recreational activities
  • Tree Limin' Extreme Ziplines
    		•  This exhilarating zipline course is situated high in the rainforest of St. Peter Mountain, where you will catch breathtaking views of Magen’s Bay.
  • Phantasea Tropical Botanical Garden
    		•  An absolutely gorgeous wildlife sanctuary with knowledgable and friendly staff. Located North-Side on the way to Mountain Top.

    Restaurants

  • Prime at Paradise Point
  		•  Prime at Paradise Point is driven by providing delicious traditional meals using the freshest, highest quality ingredients available locally and globally.
  • Fish Bar
    		•  Located on Northside right above Hull Bay Fishbar focuses on providing a fresh catch.
  • Raw Sushi
    		•  Raw's team of sushi chefs have created a menu of both traditional sushi standards and unique specialty rolls that celebrate the diverse cultures of the Caribbean.

    Rentals

  • Economy Rent a Car
  		•  Located in Nisky Center, a great place to rent a cheap island vehicle.
  • USVI Eco Tours
    		•  Enjoy the best views of the islands and its’ turquoise waters on a kayak or powerboat trip. You can even rent kayaks or stand up paddle boards and explore the lagoon on your own.
  • Coastal Watersports VI
    		•  The longest running Wave Runner rental company in St. Thomas, United States Virgin Islands. Come explore the beatiful waters with their top of the line jetski fleet.

    Hotels

  • The Westin at Frenchmans Reef.
  		•  Against a stunning backdrop of blue, you will be immersed in a dreamy, world-class resort experience. One that draws inspiration from the abundance and diversity of the natural elements and life that surrounds you.
  • Ritz-Carlton St. Thomas
    		•  A beautiful 30-acre, oceanfront luxury hotel, The Ritz-Carlton, St. Thomas is a Caribbean marvel styled after traditional island architecture, but richly updated with all of the contemporary comforts for which the resorts of The Ritz-Carlton are known.

    Shopping

  • Mango Tango Art Gallery
  		• Mango Tango art gallery was founded in 1988 by husband and wife team Jane Coombs and Smokey Pratt. with beautiful locally made artwork available for purchase.
  • Mountain Top Vi
    		•  Home of the famous Banana Daquiri. with the largest duty free gift shop in St. Thomas.
  • Vendor’s Plaza
    		•  Checkout the lively booths at Vendor's Plaza. where you’ll find local vendors selling an array of goods.
